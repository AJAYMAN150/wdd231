For Honor Compendium

Your ultimate guide to heroes, factions, maps, and strategies in For Honor.

Site Name

For Honor Compendium — chosen because it represents a comprehensive hub for players to explore all aspects of For Honor, including heroes, factions, maps, and strategies.

Site Purpose

  • Provide information on all factions: Knight, Samurai, Viking, and Wu Lin.
  • Showcase playable heroes with abilities, stats, and weapon types.
  • Offer strategy tips for duels, dominion matches, and objective play.
  • Optionally link to community events, tournaments, or news updates.

Scenarios

  • Which hero should I choose for a fast and aggressive playstyle?
  • How can I effectively counter a Warden in duels?
  • What faction is best suited for team objective play?

Color Scheme

  • Dark Gray (#1c1c1c) — background color for main sections.
  • Gold (#d4af37) — headings, section titles, buttons, and accents.
  • Red (#c0392b) — for faction warnings or alerts.

These colors are applied throughout this site plan document.

Typography

  • Roboto, sans-serif
  • Headings (h1, h2, h3): Bold, all caps, Gold color.
  • Body text: Regular weight, White, legible for readability.

Typography is applied to this document and will be used on the final site.

Wireframe

Mobile View

Stacked layout:

  • Hero cards (scrollable)
  • Faction overview
  • Strategy tips section
  • Navigation via hamburger menu at top

Desktop View

Grid layout:

  • Top navigation bar
  • Left column: Hero cards
  • Right column: Strategy tips and faction overview
  • Footer with social links and attributions

Validation & Standards

  • HTML: Valid semantic markup with header, nav, main, and footer elements.
  • CSS: Applied colors and typography from site plan, no duplicate or unused rules.
  • Accessibility: Sufficient contrast, proper heading structure, and keyboard navigation supported.
